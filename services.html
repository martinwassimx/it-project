Our Hospital Services

Emergency Care

24/7 urgent care services with immediate medical attention for accidents, injuries, and critical health conditions. Our professional emergency team ensures quick and life-saving interventions anytime needed.

Operation & Surgery

State-of-the-art operating rooms with highly skilled surgeons performing general, orthopedic, cardiac, and neurological surgeries with advanced technology and precision standards for patient safety.

Outdoor Checkup

Comprehensive outpatient consultation services including specialist advice, diagnostic tests, regular health monitoring, and preventive medical checkups to maintain your health at its best.

Ambulance Service

Fast and reliable ambulance fleet equipped with advanced life support systems, ensuring safe and quick transportation of patients to our facilities during emergencies and critical conditions.

Medicine & Pharmacy

Our in-house pharmacy ensures availability of all essential medicines, prescribed drugs, and health supplements with expert pharmacists providing professional consultation and medication guidance.

Blood Testing

Modern diagnostic labs offering a full range of blood tests including CBC, cholesterol, blood sugar, infection markers, and specialized testing with quick reporting and accurate analysis.

Dental Services

Advanced dental care units for routine cleanings, fillings, root canals, cosmetic dentistry, implants, and emergency dental treatments with experienced dentists using the latest dental technologies.

Neurology Center

Expert neurology consultations for headaches, seizures, stroke recovery, memory loss, and neurodegenerative disorders with comprehensive evaluations, imaging, and advanced treatment planning.
